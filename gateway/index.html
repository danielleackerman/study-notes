0100:00:00 — Discovery #1: Orientation

  • COREA detailed introduction to the Discovery Series — the Hemi-Sync® technology and its purpose in enhancing consciousness.

The Energy Conversion Box

  • A mental container that holds concerns, distractions, or interferences for the duration of the exercise. See it, feel it, hear it, think it, or simply imagine it.
  • Any form works — simple or ornate, high-tech or antique: a wooden chest, a sphere of light, a vacuum cleaner, a particle collider.
  • Place concrete symbols inside: a wallet for financial worry, a photo or doll for someone on your mind, limiting words like "can't," "shouldn't," "must," brick walls for your defenses, a desk or computer for work.
  • It's a tool — let it change, and return to it any time to deposit a new distraction.

Affirmation

  • Focuses your intention and attention. Use the words as spoken, modify them for personal relevance, or substitute your own. Make it yours.

Resonant Tuning

  • Accelerates the gathering of your vibrational energy while reducing internal dialogue; vitalizes and charges the whole system and loosens blockages in natural energy channels.
  • Vocalize aloud and notice any physical manifestations. The sounds on the recording are only a guide — set your own rhythm and pace, move up and down the scale, experiment with vowel sounds.

Breathing

  • Breathe only slightly deeper than normal. On the inhale, imagine pulling sparkling, vibrant energy into all parts of the body; let it move gently around the head on the hold; release tired, stale energy through the soles of the feet on each exhale.

Signposts & returning

  • Opening and closing the eyes may feel awkward — do it anyway. It's a first step in learning you can control autonomic processes and habitual behavior.
  • Return to full waking consciousness at any time by breathing normally, opening your eyes, or moving the fingers of your right hand.
  • Focus 3 is the first signpost — a Hemi-Sync state where brain and mind are more coherent, synchronized, and balanced.
  • Return to Full Waking Consciousness (C-1) at the end of every exercise is cued by a strong audio signal. Keep the headphones on until it withdraws — the more completely you come "back," the further you can go next time.

0200:33:06 — Discovery #2: Introduction to Focus 10

  • CORE"Mind Awake, Body Asleep" — the mind stays alert while the body drifts into deep rest. The first step into the Focus 10 state.
  • Follow the instructions, focusing on and then totally releasing each body part; the body knows how. "Look with your closed eyes" as you move toward profound relaxation.

The paradox of heightened sensitivity

  • Awareness can sharpen: the pores of the skin breathing, the swish of blood behind the ears, the heartbeat.
  • Spontaneous remote sensing is possible — sounds or faraway, delicate odors. Place all expectations in the box, relax, and go with whatever you experience.

Health Affirmation

  • Closing each exercise, it helps balance physical and nonphysical energies — working for you without effort on your part.

0301:10:16 — Discovery #3: Advanced Focus 10

  • COREBuild on the basic practice, deepening the ability to hold awareness in this profound state.

The Resonant Energy Balloon (REBAL)

  • A tool for creating a high-energy state within and around you. The REBAL acts as a magnet — attracting influences you desire and shielding you from undesired energy. The more you build it, the better you sustain higher energy.
  • It may spontaneously change form, develop a delicate membrane, or grow radiant like an intensified aura. Even when you don't perceive it, play with sensing it, or store the energy as in a charged battery.
  • Switch it on in C-1 or any state with a single resonant energy breath: inhale vibrant energy, and as you hold it, picture a bright moving circle with the number 10 inside; let the circle surround you as you exhale.
  • Experiment — "pop" your REBAL in a group and see whether its resonance attracts people in harmony with it; extend it around your car and note whether traffic and parking come easier.
